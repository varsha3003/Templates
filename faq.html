FAQ General Questions you may have

1:To whom the Semester Based Credit and Grading System applicable?

The Semester Based Credit and Grading System is applicable only to the learners admitted(enrolled) for the first year of the Undergraduate degree programmes from academic year 2011‐2012 onwards to the affiliated colleges and University Departments It is applicable to both traditional & self financing undergraduate degree programmes under the faculty of Arts, Science & Commerce i.e. B.A., B. Com., B.Sc., B.M.S., B. Com (Banking & Insurance), B.Com (Accounting & Finance), B. Com. (Financial Market), B. Sc. (Information Technology), and B.M.M. etc.  

2:Is the issue of Photocopy and Revaluation applicable to the Internal Assessment?   

Photocopy and Revaluation is not applicable to Internal Assessment as per the existing VCD of the University of Mumbai.

3:When and how should the Internal Assessment be conducted and evaluated?

The respective Colleges will have to schedule for conduct of class test and assignment and/or project. The respective teacher shall submit the marks obtained by the learner in the internal assessment (40 marks) to the examination committee prior to the conduct of semester end examination.
